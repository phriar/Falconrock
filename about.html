About FalconRock

Built in the
Server Room,

FalconRock wasn't built on business plans and pitch decks. It grew out of 25 years spent inside the most demanding IT environments in the federal government — solving the problems others passed on.

Our Story

From Hands-On Engineer
to Independent Consultancy

FalconRock's founder started where all good infrastructure engineers start — in the server room. Early career VDI migrations that nobody else wanted to touch. Network builds in classified facilities where the margin for error was zero. Complex virtualization deployments that senior engineers declined because the constraints were too tight.

Over two decades, that willingness to take on the hardest work built deep expertise across virtualization, network architecture, systems engineering, and security compliance — all within cleared DoD environments. FalconRock Consulting is the natural evolution of that career: an independent firm that brings the same operational depth to every engagement.

Today, we're extending that foundation into on-premises AI — helping organizations that need the power of modern language models without the risk of cloud data exposure.

Foundation Years

Virtualization & VDI

Built a reputation on enterprise virtualization work — the complex migrations and deployments that required patience, precision, and deep technical skill. Earned trust by delivering in environments where others struggled.

Growth

Network & Systems Architecture

Expanded into full-stack systems and network architecture across classified programs. Became the go-to resource for cross-domain designs, enclave networking, and infrastructure at scale.

Senior Leadership

Cleared Program Architecture

Established as a senior cleared architect across multiple high-value defense programs. Took on the role of critical technical lead — the person the program couldn't run without.

Present

FalconRock Consulting

Launched FalconRock to deliver that same senior-level expertise independently — now expanding into on-premises AI solutions for organizations that can't put their data in the cloud.

Industries We Serve

Who We Work With

Our primary focus is Department of Defense and federal civilian agencies. We have extensive experience supporting programs across combatant commands, defense agencies, and the intelligence community — always in roles that require active security clearances and deep technical ownership.

We're also bringing our on-premises AI expertise to regulated private-sector organizations — particularly law firms, financial services, and healthcare organizations that need AI capabilities but can't risk putting sensitive client data into cloud-based services.

Whether it's a cleared enclave or a privacy-sensitive practice, the common thread is the same: environments where data security and system reliability aren't optional.
